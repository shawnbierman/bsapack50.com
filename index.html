Cub Scout Pack 50

Collierville, TN

Welcome to Pack 50!

Founded in 1967, Pack 50 is a non-profit organization chartered by Collierville United Methodist Church in Collierville, TN. We are a family-friendly Cub Scout pack dedicated to teaching the ideals of Cub Scouts to boys in grades 1-5.

Pack meetings are held at Collierville United Methodist Church traditionally on the 3rd Monday of every month during the school year at 6:30 p.m. Hope to see you there!

Our mission is based on the Scout Oath
On my honor I will do my best
To do my duty to God and my country

and to obey the Scout Law;
To help other people at all times;

To keep myself physically strong,
mentally awake, and morally straight.
